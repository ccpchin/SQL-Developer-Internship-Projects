# Employee Management and Attendance Tracker

This PostgreSQL-backed project tracks employee details, departmental structure, and daily attendance while providing reports and predictive attrition analysis. The solution includes triggers, functions, and analytics relevant to HR systems.

## Key Features:
- PostgreSQL schema with `Departments`, `Roles`, `Employees`, and `Attendance` tables.
- Trigger to auto-fill attendance status.
- Simulated multi-day attendance with `Present`, `Late`, and `Absent` tags.
- Grouped reports showing employee attendance percentages.
- Function to compute total working hours per employee.
- Machine Learning model to predict employee attrition based on HR dataset.

Designed for internships and resume projects, this system emphasizes database-driven logic, real-world HR analytics, and structured PostgreSQL usage.
